Italian food vocabulary

The 60 words that carry you through every food situation in Italy.

At the bar (coffee)

  • caffè — espresso (never say "espresso" — it's just "caffè")
  • cappuccino — but never after 11am if you don't want a puzzled look
  • caffè macchiato / latte macchiato — spot of milk in coffee / spot of coffee in milk
  • cornetto — croissant
  • brioche — sweet bun (varies by region)
  • spremuta — freshly squeezed juice
  • acqua naturale / frizzante — still / sparkling water

At the restaurant

  • antipasto — starter / primo — first (pasta) / secondo — main / contorno — side / dolce — dessert
  • coperto — cover charge (yes, it's normal, just pay it)
  • bicchiere — glass / bottiglia — bottle
  • vino rosso / bianco / rosato — red / white / rosé wine
  • Il conto, per favore. — The bill, please.

At the supermarket

  • pane bread / latte milk / uova eggs / burro butter / formaggio cheese
  • frutta fruit: mela, pera, arancia, uva, banana, fragola
  • verdura vegetables: pomodoro, insalata, patata, zucchina, melanzana, peperone
  • carne meat / pesce fish / pollo chicken / maiale pork / manzo beef
  • riso rice / pasta pasta / olio oil / sale salt / pepe pepper

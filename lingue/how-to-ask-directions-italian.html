How to ask for directions in Italian

Getting lost in an Italian city is inevitable. Here's how to get unlost.

Download on the App Store

Asking

  • Scusi, dov'è la stazione? — Excuse me, where is the station?
  • Come si arriva al Colosseo? — How do I get to the Colosseum?
  • Mi può indicare la strada per…? — Can you show me the way to…?
  • È lontano? — Is it far?
  • A che ora chiude? — What time does it close?

Understanding the answer

  • a destra / a sinistra — to the right / to the left
  • sempre dritto — straight on
  • giri a destra — turn right (formal) / gira a destra (informal)
  • attraversi la piazza — cross the square
  • di fronte a… — in front of / opposite
  • vicino a… / lontano da… — near / far from
  • all'angolo — at the corner
  • al semaforo — at the traffic light

When you didn't understand

  • Può ripetere? — Can you repeat?
  • Più lentamente, per favore. — More slowly, please.
  • Non ho capito. — I didn't understand.
  • Mi può scrivere l'indirizzo? — Can you write down the address for me?

Italian house vocabulary

Rooms, furniture, and the phrases for finding a place to live in Italy.

Download on the App Store

The rooms

  • casa home / appartamento apartment / villa detached house
  • ingresso entrance hallway / corridoio corridor
  • soggiorno / salotto living room / cucina kitchen
  • camera (da letto) bedroom / bagno bathroom
  • studio study / balcone balcony / terrazzo terrace
  • giardino garden / cantina cellar / garage garage

Furniture and appliances

  • tavolo table / sedia chair / divano sofa / poltrona armchair
  • letto bed / armadio wardrobe / comodino nightstand
  • frigorifero (frigo) fridge / lavatrice washing machine / lavastoviglie dishwasher
  • forno oven / fornelli stove burners

Renting an apartment

  • Cerco un appartamento in affitto. — I'm looking for an apartment to rent.
  • Quanto costa l'affitto? — How much is the rent?
  • Le spese sono incluse? — Are utilities included?
  • Quanto è la caparra? — How much is the deposit?
  • Il contratto è di quanto tempo? — How long is the contract?

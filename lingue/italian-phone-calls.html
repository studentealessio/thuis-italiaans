How to make a phone call in Italian

No visual cues, background noise, sometimes bad connection — phone calls are hard. Here's your script.

Download on the App Store

Answering and starting

  • Pronto? — Hello? (only used when answering the phone)
  • Buongiorno, sono Marco. — Good morning, this is Marco.
  • Vorrei parlare con la dottoressa Rossi. — I'd like to speak with Dr. Rossi.
  • Chi parla? — Who's speaking? (they may ask)

When you don't understand

  • Scusi, non ho sentito bene. Può ripetere? — Sorry, I didn't hear. Can you repeat?
  • Non parlo italiano molto bene, può parlare più lentamente? — I don't speak Italian very well, could you speak more slowly?
  • Mi può richiamare più tardi? — Could you call me back later?

Ending

  • Grazie mille, arrivederci. — Thanks very much, goodbye.
  • A presto. — See you soon.
  • Buona giornata. — Have a good day.

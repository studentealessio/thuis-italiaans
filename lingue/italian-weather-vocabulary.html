Italian weather vocabulary

The weather is the #1 small-talk topic in Italy. Here's how to hold your own.

Download on the App Store

The vocabulary

  • sole sun / pioggia rain / neve snow / vento wind / nebbia fog
  • nuvole clouds / temporale thunderstorm / tempesta storm
  • caldo hot / freddo cold / fresco cool / umido humid

Real phrases Italians use

  • Che tempo fa? — What's the weather like?
  • Fa caldo / freddo / fresco. — It's hot / cold / cool. (Notice: fare, not essere.)
  • C'è il sole. — It's sunny.
  • Piove. / Nevica. / Tira vento. — It's raining / snowing / windy.
  • È una bella giornata. — It's a lovely day.
  • Che caldo! — How hot it is!

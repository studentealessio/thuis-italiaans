The patterns that give you free words

  • English -tion → Italian -zione: nation → nazione, station → stazione, education → educazione, situation → situazione. This alone gives you hundreds of words.
  • English -ty → Italian -tà: liberty → libertà, university → università, city → città, quality → qualità.
  • English -ous → Italian -oso: famous → famoso, delicious → delizioso, curious → curioso, generous → generoso.
  • English -ic / -ical → Italian -ico: musical → musicale, historic → storico, tragic → tragico, tropical → tropicale.
  • English -ent / -ant → Italian -ente / -ante: important → importante, evident → evidente, elegant → elegante.
  • English -ive → Italian -ivo: active → attivo, negative → negativo, positive → positivo.

Perfect matches (no change needed)

  • Music: opera, sonata, aria, tempo, forte, piano, viola, violino
  • Food: pasta, pizza, tiramisù, gelato, mozzarella, prosciutto, panino, biscotto
  • Art: fresco, stucco, graffiti, chiaroscuro, studio, replica
  • Everyday: bravo, casino, banca, area, radio, taxi, hotel

The false-friend traps to remember

The patterns above get you thousands of free words. But watch:

  • simpatico ≠ sympathetic → friendly
  • attualmente ≠ actually → currently
  • libreria ≠ library → bookshop
  • fabbrica ≠ fabric → factory
  • caldo ≠ cold → hot

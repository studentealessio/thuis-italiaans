Italian Graded Readers · B1

Intermediate Italian books (level B1)

B1 is the threshold of independence, and these books are built for it: longer sentences, the present subjunctive, real hypothetical clauses and the discourse connectors that hold Italian prose together. They are ideal reading practice if you attend an intermediate course or are preparing a B1 certification (CILS, CELI, PLIDA) — each chapter ends with comprehension questions, just like an exam task, but attached to a story you actually want to finish.

Most titles are free with Kindle Unlimited · ebook $4.99 · also in paperback

On this page: Il fu Mattia Pascal — a puzzle plot about a man believed dead; Canne al vento — Nobel-winning prose from Sardinia; Il paese di Cuccagna — choral Naples in the grip of lottery fever; Il servitore di due padroni — Goldoni's comedy, all dialogue and misunderstandings; Orlando furioso — Ariosto's flying horse and the trip to the Moon.

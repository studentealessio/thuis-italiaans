Learn to speak Italian like a native with our specialized course, featuring phonetics, native speaker audio, and more.

Ciao a tutti! Pronunciation is key to mastering any language, and Italian is no exception. Our Italian Pronunciation Practice courses are specifically designed to help you speak like a native, focusing on clarity and accuracy.

Courses Highlights

  • Phonetics and Intonation: Learn the nuances of Italian sounds and rhythm. Master the melodic nature of Italian and understand the importance of stress and intonation.
  • Native Speaker Audio: Practice with recordings from native speakers to perfect your accent. Listen to various regional accents to gain a well-rounded understanding of Italian pronunciation.
  • Interactive Exercises: Engage in exercises that improve your pronunciation through repetition and feedback. Participate in live pronunciation drills and receive instant corrections.

Why Our Course Stands Out: With a strong academic background in language teaching and years of experience, I offer tailored lessons that cater to individual needs. My ongoing studies in ITALS at Ca' Foscari Venezia University further enrich my teaching methods, ensuring you receive the best instruction possible.

Living in Italy: Immersive Learning

If you're fortunate enough to live in Italy, your learning experience transcends traditional classroom settings. Engage with native speakers daily, immerse yourself in Italian traditions, and let the language become a natural part of your routine. The vibrant culture and rich history of Italy will surround you, making your learning journey both practical and culturally enriching. Through active participation in social life and consistent practice in class, you'll master Italian from both formal and informal perspectives, gaining a deep understanding of the language and its cultural context.

Additional Resources: Access a variety of supplementary materials, including pronunciation guides, practice worksheets, and online forums where you can practice with fellow learners.

Italian Classes

Expertly Crafted Italian Courses with Alessio

24 settembre 2023

"Learn Italian from Home" offers expertly designed, academically informed Italian classes tailored to diverse learner needs.

Dive into the rich tapestry of Italian language and culture with our expertly designed courses at "Learn Italian from Home." Guided by the deep academic insights and innovative teaching methodologies from my ongoing Master’s studies in Teaching and Promoting the Italian Language and Culture to Foreigners (ITALS) at Ca’ Foscari Venezia University, each course is crafted to meet the nuanced needs of learners both in Italy and abroad.

Whether you're encountering Italian for the first time, looking to deepen your linguistic skills, or needing to integrate into the Italian community, our courses are designed to provide comprehensive linguistic and cultural education.

Italian Culture and Language for Beginners and Advanced Learners

Inspired by my ITALS studies, this course offers two tracks: one for learners within Italy (L2) and one for those abroad (LS), each addressing the specific context of the learners. This structure ensures that all students, regardless of their geographic location, receive education that’s relevant to their learning environment.

Business Italian

Utilizing innovative methodologies from my master's coursework, this program is designed for professionals who require a command of Italian to navigate the business landscapes in and outside of Italy. The course includes specialized vocabulary and scenarios that reflect the contemporary Italian business environment.

Italian for Children and/or Immigrants

Reflecting the needs highlighted in my studies, this course offers specialized paths for young learners and immigrants, facilitating their integration and helping them navigate the challenges of acclimating to a new culture.

Why Choose Our Italian Online Courses?

At "Learn Italian from Home," we understand that the landscape of Italian learners has dramatically shifted. Traditional methods are no longer sufficient. Our courses reflect the latest in glottodidactic approaches, focusing on adult learning, integration strategies for immigrants, and the spread of Italian as a vibrant second language globally. With a focus on promoting Italian akin to major European languages, we also incorporate necessary marketing savvy into language learning, making it more accessible and appealing.

Whether you are looking to embrace Italian for personal enrichment, professional advancement, or cultural integration, "Learn Italian from Home" offers you a gateway into a world of effective and enjoyable language learning. Sign up today and start your journey with an educational partner who is deeply invested in your learning success, backed by rigorous academic training and a passion for Italian language and culture. Join us, and let's explore the beauty of Italian together!

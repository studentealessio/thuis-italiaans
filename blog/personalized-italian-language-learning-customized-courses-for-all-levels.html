Personalized Italian courses with tailored learning, dynamic methods, cultural immersion, and supportive community for all levels.

Are you ready to embark on a journey into the Italian language that’s crafted just for you? Whether you're a total beginner or looking to advance your proficiency, our personalized Italian language courses offer a learning experience designed to fit your individual needs and goals. This isn’t just any language course; it's a gateway to a deeper understanding of Italian culture and conversation, tailored specifically for you by a seasoned expert in the field.

Meet Your Italian Teacher

Hello, I'm your dedicated guide to all things Italian! With over five years of experience teaching Italian both online and in-person in Amsterdam, I bring a wealth of knowledge and passion to our classes. My academic background is rich with expertise, featuring a Double Degree in Applied Languages, a Master’s in Project Management from Rome Business School, and a Master's in Teaching Italian as a Second Language from IPSEF.

But that’s not all—I’m currently enriching my qualifications with a Master's in Teaching and Promoting the Italian Language and Culture to Foreigners (ITALS) at Ca’ Foscari Venezia University. I also serve as a teacher assistant at a private school in Amsterdam. My approach is shaped by these experiences, ensuring that each course is not only educational but also deeply rooted in real-world applications.

How Our Italian Courses Stand Out

Tailored Learning Experiences: Each course is uniquely designed around your personal goals, proficiency level, and learning style. I offer individual and group classes, ensuring that whether you need one-on-one attention or thrive in a group setting, your learning needs are met.

Dynamic Teaching Methods: Forget monotonous lessons; our classes include a dynamic mix of multimedia resources, real-life materials, and interactive sessions. This blend ensures that each lesson is engaging and informative, making learning Italian as enjoyable as it is effective.

Cultural Immersion: Learning a language is incomplete without understanding its cultural context. That’s why our courses incorporate significant cultural elements, from history to current events in Italy, enhancing your learning experience and providing you with a holistic view of Italian life.

A Supportive Learning Community: Join our vibrant community of learners from all around the world. Currently, I mentor a fantastic group of 10 returning students, each bringing their unique perspectives and motivations to our classes, enriching the learning experience for everyone.

Course Offerings

Beginner to Advanced Levels: No matter your starting point, there’s a course for you. Each level is carefully crafted to challenge and encourage growth in your language skills.

Specialized Courses: From conversational Italian to business Italian, each specialized course is designed to meet specific needs, whether you're planning to travel to Italy or engage in business with Italian clients.

Children’s Italian: Tailored programs for young learners make learning Italian fun and engaging, setting them up for a multilingual future.

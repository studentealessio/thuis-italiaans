Benvenuti!

Planning a trip to Italy? Our Italian for Travel courses are designed to equip you with the essential language skills you need to navigate your travels with ease.

Travel-Focused Learning

  • Essential Phrases: Learn key phrases and expressions for travel. From greetings to emergency phrases, get prepared for any situation.
  • Situational Practice: Practice Italian for common travel scenarios such as dining, shopping, and transportation. Role-play various scenarios to gain confidence.
  • Cultural Tips: Gain insights into Italian customs and etiquette to enhance your travel experience. Learn how to blend in with the locals and make the most of your trip.

Why Learn with Me? With extensive teaching experience and advanced studies in language instruction, I bring a wealth of knowledge and practical experience to my courses. Currently pursuing a Master's in ITALS at Ca' Foscari Venezia University and working as a teacher assistant, I ensure each lesson is practical and engaging.

Interactive and Engaging Methods

  • Role Play and Situational Exercises: Practice real-life scenarios to build confidence and fluency.
  • Conversational Practice: Engage in guided improvisation, role plays, and situational exercises to ensure you can handle any situation while traveling.
  • Tailor-Made Exercises: Exercises tailored to your travel plans and interests, making learning fun and effective.

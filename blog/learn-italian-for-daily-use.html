Italian language teacher with extensive academic background offers personalized, immersive Italian courses for mastering Italian in everyday contexts.

Buongiorno!

As an Italian language teacher with extensive academic and practical experience, I specialize in crafting personalized learning experiences that help you master Italian for everyday use. My approach is rooted in a deep academic understanding of language acquisition and is driven by my passion to make language learning accessible and enjoyable for all.

My Educational Background and Teaching Philosophy

I embarked on my educational journey with a firm resolve to bring the Italian language and culture closer to people worldwide. My academic path includes a double degree in Applied Languages, two Master’s degrees—one in Project Management and another in Teaching Italian as a Second Language from Rome Business School and IPSEF, respectively. Additionally, I am currently pursuing another Master's at Ca’ Foscari Venezia University in Teaching and Promoting Italian Language and Culture to Foreigners.

This advanced training underscores the dual aspects of my teaching approach: teaching Italian to foreigners living in Italy and to those learning from abroad. This extensive education has equipped me with cutting-edge techniques and a modern understanding of the needs of today’s Italian learners, who seek practical language skills that can be applied in a variety of everyday contexts.

Unique Aspects of My Italian Language Courses

Adaptive Learning Methods: My teaching methodology adapts to the evolving landscape of language education, utilizing innovative and customized teaching approaches. Whether you need language skills for professional purposes or personal enrichment, the courses are designed to meet your specific needs and learning pace.

Contextual Learning: Leveraging real-world materials—such as multimedia resources, interactive digital content, and contemporary Italian literature—my courses offer a comprehensive learning experience that goes beyond conventional textbook methods. We engage with materials created for and by Italians, allowing for a richer and more authentic learning experience.

Cultural Integration: My courses do not just teach the language; they immerse you in Italian culture, helping you understand the contextual uses of the language in various social and professional scenarios. This holistic approach not only enhances linguistic skills but also deepens cultural appreciation.

Tailored Learning Paths

No matter where you are in your Italian learning journey, I provide tailored courses that align with your current proficiency and goals. For students in Italy, our classes leverage everyday interactions and societal engagement as core elements of language education. For students abroad, we focus on building strong foundational skills and cultural understanding, preparing you for future interactions or visits to Italy.

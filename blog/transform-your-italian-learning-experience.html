The Impact of Multicultural Contexts in Learning Italian

Learning Italian at Ca’ Foscari isn't just about mastering grammar and vocabulary; it's about immersing yourself in an environment that values cultural diversity and understanding. The teaching methodology ensures that each lesson is not only a language session but also a cultural dialogue, enhancing both linguistic and cultural competencies simultaneously.

What Makes Ca’ Foscari’s Method Unique?

Our courses are structured around comprehensive units that are complete and coherent yet interconnected, providing a seamless learning journey from start to finish. These units are designed to motivate and engage you through a variety of channels, ensuring that every class contributes to a strategic, curriculum-wide goal. This method helps in retaining consistency in learning and ensures that every lesson builds on the previous one to reinforce and expand your language skills.

Real-World Applications

The practical application of what you learn in our Italian courses is facilitated by an emphasis on real-world usage and intercultural communication skills. This approach is particularly beneficial if you aim to use Italian in a professional or social context, making you not just a speaker, but a communicator.

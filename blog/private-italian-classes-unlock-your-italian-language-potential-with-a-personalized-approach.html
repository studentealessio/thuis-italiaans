Private Italian Classes: Unlock Your Italian Language Potential with a Personalized Approach

5 febbraio 2023

Private Italian classes offer personalized, flexible learning with authentic materials and a dedicated instructor for fluency.

Are you looking to dive deep into the Italian language and culture with a learning experience tailored specifically for you? Private Italian classes offer a unique, personalized approach to language learning that caters to your individual needs, goals, and pace. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to polish your fluency, a private class setting ensures that your educational journey is as rewarding as it is effective.

Meet Italian Teacher: A Maestro of Italian Linguistics

Imagine learning Italian from someone who's not just passionate about the language, but also deeply immersed in teaching it. Your instructor has been guiding students through the intricacies of Italian for over five years, both online and in-person in Amsterdam. With a Double Degree in Applied Languages and not one, but two Master's degrees—one in Project Management from Rome Business School and another in Teaching Italian as a Second Language from IPSEF—your educational journey is in expert hands.

But that’s not all. As the icing on the cake (or should we say, ciliegina sulla torta), your instructor is currently pursuing another Master's in Teaching and Promoting the Italian Language and Culture to Foreigners at Ca’ Foscari Venezia University, while also assisting in teaching at a private school in Amsterdam. With this depth of academic and practical experience, each lesson is crafted to be as enlightening as it is enjoyable.

Personalized Learning Paths: Tailored to Your Needs

Every learner is unique, and so should be the learning approach. Depending on your current proficiency and goals—which we'll determine together—you will embark on a personalized course. Expect reachable homework and an instructor’s undivided attention. No more one-size-fits-all textbooks; your classes will use a dynamic mix of multimedia resources, from PDFs to videos and original Italian articles, enhancing both language skills and cultural understanding.

Experience Italy Through Authentic Materials

Say goodbye to mundane lessons. Our course material is specially chosen to reflect genuine Italian usage, meant for native speakers. This not only improves your language proficiency but also deepens your cultural appreciation, making you feel closer to the everyday life and vibrant culture of Italy. It's not just about learning a language; it's about experiencing a culture.

Flexible and Engaging Class Formats

Classes are designed to be flexible, accommodating your schedule and learning preferences. Whether you prefer the interaction of video calls or the convenience of asynchronous task-based assignments, the goal is to make your learning practical, engaging, and aligned with the CEFR standards for language proficiency.

Ready to embark on a language journey that's crafted just for you? Private Italian classes are more than just lessons; they're a gateway to a richer understanding and appreciation of Italian culture. With a tailored approach and a dedicated instructor, your path to fluency will be as fulfilling as it is fun. Let’s get started on this exciting educational adventure!

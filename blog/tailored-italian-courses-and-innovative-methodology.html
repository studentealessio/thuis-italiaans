Italian Classes

Tailored Italian Courses and Innovative Methodology

1 ottobre 2023

"Learn Italian from Home" offers diverse Italian classes with a unique methodology for effective and enjoyable language learning.

Are you ready to embark on a delightful journey into the Italian language and culture? Whether you're looking to connect with your Italian roots, prepare for an Italian adventure, or simply add a new skill to your repertoire, you've come to the perfect place.

At "Learn Italian from Home," we offer a variety of courses designed to meet the unique needs and goals of each learner:

Italian Culture Course: This course is ideal for those who wish to gain a deep understanding of Italian traditions, art, history, and everyday life. It builds a solid foundation in the basics of the language while immersing students in the cultural aspects of Italy.

Conversational Italian: Focused on enhancing verbal skills, this course allows students to practice Italian through discussions on a variety of topics such as life, hobbies, and global events, ensuring they can confidently communicate in Italian in any setting.

Business Italian: Tailored for professionals, this course covers specialized vocabulary and situational practice for business environments, perfect for those looking to use Italian in their careers.

Italian for Children: Designed with young learners in mind, this course uses engaging methods like games and interactive activities to make learning Italian fun and effective, setting a strong linguistic foundation early on.

Learning Italian Methodology

Our educational approach is structured around four key phases that ensure comprehensive learning:

Motivation: We ignite students' passion for learning Italian by making initial encounters with the language exciting and engaging, fostering a positive attitude and a strong initial connection with the language.

Globality: Our courses provide a broad understanding of Italian, setting a context that includes cultural, historical, and social aspects. This helps students see the language as a living, dynamic tool for communication and cultural appreciation.

Analysis: In this phase, we encourage detailed exploration of the language, where students dissect and examine Italian in depth. This builds critical thinking and analytical skills, crucial for mastering the language.

Synthesis: Finally, we focus on synthesis, where students integrate what they've learned into a cohesive whole. This phase is about applying knowledge in practical, real-world contexts, enhancing creativity and problem-solving skills.

Combining Italian Courses and Learning Methodology

Our courses and methodology are intricately linked to provide a seamless learning experience. Each course is designed to guide students through the four phases of our methodology:

In the Italian Culture Course, motivation is sparked through engaging cultural content, analysis through the study of cultural texts and practices, and synthesis through projects that require a deep understanding of cultural contexts.

The Conversational Italian course uses real-life scenarios to motivate students, focuses on the analysis of conversational structures, and encourages synthesis through the practical use of language in discussions and debates.

Business Italian prepares professionals by motivating them with the potential for career enhancement, analyzing business-specific language structures, and synthesizing these skills in simulations of business negotiations and presentations.

Italian for Children motivates with fun activities, analyzes through age-appropriate linguistic exercises, and synthesizes knowledge through interactive storytelling and role-play.

"Learn Italian from Home" uniquely combines specialized courses with a progressive methodology to ensure that learning Italian is as effective as it is enjoyable. Each course is thoughtfully designed to move students from initial curiosity to complete linguistic and cultural competence, making the learning journey both comprehensive and deeply engaging.

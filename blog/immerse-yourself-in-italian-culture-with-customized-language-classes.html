Welcome to our Italian learning program, where every class is personalized to fit your unique goals and interests!

Are you eager to dive into the Italian language and culture in a way that's specifically designed for you? Our courses are not just lessons; they are stepping stones to a comprehensive cultural experience. Here, we believe that a personalized approach is key to effective learning. That's why we say, "Every class is tailored to you!"

Customized Italian Learning Journey

From the outset, we work together to set your learning objectives. Your course is tailored to these goals, with manageable homework and my undivided attention throughout your journey. Forget about one-size-fits-all learning; our approach ensures that your educational experience is as unique as you are.

Dynamic and Engaging Italian Materials

Get ready for an engaging learning journey that transcends traditional textbooks. Our courses are designed to captivate your interest through a dynamic blend of multimedia resources. Wave goodbye to monotony as we explore Italy with PDFs, videos, and articles sourced directly from Italian media. This approach not only enhances your language skills but also ensures that your learning is deeply rooted in contemporary Italian culture.

Experience Real Italian Use

We tailor the materials to your proficiency level, from beginners to advanced learners. By using resources originally intended for an Italian audience, we provide a learning experience that’s both language-focused and culturally enriching. My goal is to make your language journey educational, stimulating, and incredibly exciting.

Italian Courses Designed for Every Learner

  1. Italian Culture: Begin with the basics and establish a solid foundation in Italian. This tailor-made program helps you not only learn the language but immerse yourself in Italian culture.
  2. Conversational Italian: Discuss life, hobbies, and more in our conversational courses. We focus on helping you express ideas and accomplish tasks in Italian through guided improvisation, role plays, and situational exercises.
  3. Business Italian: Elevate your language skills to discuss complex topics. We prepare you to articulate abstract ideas and understand deeper cultural and social dynamics in Italy.
  4. Italian for Children: Designed for young learners, this course builds a strong linguistic foundation, promoting bilingual or trilingual capabilities through playful learning and recognition of linguistic patterns.

Italian Learning Goals with the CEFR

Our courses align with the CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages), providing a standardized assessment of your language skills. This global framework helps you understand and measure your proficiency in a clear and structured way.

Enhance Your Italian with Our Expert-Led Online Courses

27 agosto 2023

"Learn Italian from Home" offers advanced, interactive Italian courses tailored for all levels, emphasizing practicality and engagement.

At "Learn Italian from Home," we pride ourselves on offering state-of-the-art Italian courses online, tailored for learners of all levels. Our curriculum is deeply influenced by my extensive training in the Master’s program in "Teaching Italian L2 to Foreigners: Methodology and Didactics" at Università Telematica Giustino Fortunato. This comprehensive training enriches our courses, ensuring they are not only academically robust but also practically applicable in various everyday scenarios.

My recent academic journey covered over 1500 hours and focused on cutting-edge linguistic and pedagogical strategies essential for teaching Italian effectively. Key areas of study included:

Glottology and Linguistics: Understanding the intricate structures and functions of the Italian language.

Modern Language Teaching: Applying contemporary methods that enhance language acquisition.

Italian Linguistics: Diving deep into the grammatical, sociolinguistic, and historical aspects of Italian.

These elements are vital in developing courses that not only teach Italian but also engage students in a meaningful exploration of the language.

We offer several specialized online Italian classes that utilize this high-level training:

General Italian Courses: From beginners to advanced levels, these courses integrate the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR), ensuring a structured and progressive learning path.

Specialized Italian Courses: For those interested in specific areas such as business Italian or cultural immersion, our courses use tailored content that applies advanced teaching methodologies, making learning practical and relevant.

Italian for Educators and Professionals: Particularly beneficial for teachers and facilitators, this course focuses on strategies for teaching Italian as a second language, with a strong emphasis on communicative and intercultural approaches.

The methodologies I studied emphasize communicative teaching, which focuses on interaction as both the means and the ultimate goal of learning Italian. This approach is particularly effective in online settings, where engagement can be challenging to maintain. Our courses are designed to be interactive, incorporating multimedia resources like videos, audios, and real-time conversations, which are crucial for fostering a dynamic learning environment.

Join us at "Learn Italian from Home" to experience a superior form of language learning. Our courses are enriched by advanced pedagogical training, tailored to help you achieve fluency and confidence in Italian. Whether you are learning Italian for personal growth, professional development, or educational purposes, our courses are designed to meet your needs effectively and efficiently.

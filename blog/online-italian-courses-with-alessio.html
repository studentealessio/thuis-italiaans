Online Italian Courses With Alessio

17 settembre 2023

Learn Italian from Home offers advanced Italian courses, combining academic rigor with practical teaching methods for effective learning.

Ciao, ciao, ciao !

I am excited to leverage my recent academic achievements to enhance our Italian language courses further. I've successfully completed an intensive Master’s program in "Teaching Italian L2 to Foreigners: Methodology and Didactics," accredited by the MIUR (Italian Ministry of Education, University and Research) and offered in collaboration with the Università Telematica Giustino Fortunato. This specialized training is designed to equip educators like myself with the latest pedagogical strategies and deep insights into teaching Italian as a second or foreign language.

Integrating Advanced Educational Methodologies

The Master’s program has armed me with cutting-edge techniques and a profound understanding of how to effectively teach Italian, tailoring lessons to the communicative needs of learners. This approach goes beyond traditional language teaching by focusing on practical language use, ensuring students not only learn Italian but can actively use it in real-world scenarios. Here’s how this advanced training shapes our courses:

Italian Culture class: Enhanced with methodologies that blend cultural immersion with language learning, ensuring students not only learn Italian in theory but also understand its application within Italian culture.

Conversational Italian class: Structured around communicative methods to foster real conversational skills, making lessons highly interactive and responsive to student needs.

Business Italian class: Tailored to include specific business vocabulary and scenarios, this course now integrates more dynamic teaching methods to facilitate professional communication in Italian.

Italian for Children class: Adopts child-friendly teaching techniques that make learning engaging and effective for young minds, focusing on interactive and fun approaches to language acquisition.

The comprehensive training I received is directly relevant to our diverse online offerings at "Learn Italian from Home." It equips me with the ability to address the varied and evolving needs of learners worldwide, whether they are professionals looking to expand their business skills, immigrants adjusting to life in Italy, or children starting their journey into bilingualism. The Master's program has provided me with the tools to enhance the structural, syntactic, and glottological aspects of the Italian language, ensuring each course we offer is not only educational but also enriching.

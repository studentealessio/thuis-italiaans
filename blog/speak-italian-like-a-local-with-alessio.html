Ciao!

I'm your dedicated Italian language teacher, bringing over five years of rich experience and specialized education to help you speak Italian as naturally as if you were born here. Whether you're planning to enjoy Italy's rich cultural heritage up close or from afar, I design language learning experiences that are as enjoyable as they are effective.

My Journey to Teaching Excellence

My journey in language education began with a deep passion for sharing the beauty of the Italian language and culture with the world. This passion led me to pursue not just one, but two Master's degrees focused on the methodology and didactics of teaching Italian as a second language and as a foreign language. I studied intensively at the prestigious Ca' Foscari University of Venice and the IPSEF in Milan, focusing on two key paths: teaching Italian to foreigners residing in Italy and teaching Italian abroad.

The advanced training I received was based on the recognition that the demographics of Italian learners have evolved significantly over the years. Traditional training methods no longer suffice due to rapidly advancing glottodidactic approaches and the need for tailored teaching methodologies, especially for adult learners. Italian is no longer seen merely as an immigrant language but is recognized globally as a critical second foreign language after English.

What Sets My Teaching Apart?

Innovative Teaching Approaches: My courses are crafted with the latest in language teaching innovations, especially those designed for teaching adults. These methods are dynamic and responsive, catering to the unique needs of each student, whether they are immigrants needing integration support or professionals seeking to advance their careers.

Real-World Application: I utilize a variety of real-life materials and scenarios in my courses, moving beyond traditional textbooks. We might use video clips from Italian news, discussions on current social issues, or explore Italian art and literature, all designed to provide a holistic and practical use of the language.

Community and Sharing: A key component of my teaching philosophy is building a community of learners who share experiences, methodologies, and challenges. This approach not only enriches the learning environment but also helps in creating a supportive network that mimics the natural ways through which languages are learned and used.

Tailored Courses for Every Need

Whether you are a complete beginner or an advanced learner, my courses are tailored to your level of proficiency and personal goals. For those in Italy, we focus on integrating language learning with daily interactions to facilitate natural acquisition of the language. For my international students, the approach includes structured lessons that are rich in cultural content and practical application, preparing you for real-life use or future travels to Italy.

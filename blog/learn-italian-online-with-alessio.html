Hello, Hola, Ciao!

If you're looking to dive deep into the Italian language and embrace its rich culture, you've come to the right place! I'm thrilled to guide you through your Italian learning journey with our specially tailored courses at "Learn Italian from Home." With over five years of experience teaching Italian both online and in person in Amsterdam, to individuals and groups, I bring a wealth of knowledge and a passion for teaching that will make your learning experience both effective and enjoyable.

My Background: A Rich Blend of Education and Experience

Armed with a Double Degree in Applied Languages and not one, but two Master's degrees, I have a solid academic foundation in language education. Currently, I am pursuing another Master's in Teaching and Promoting the Italian Language and Culture to Foreigners (ITALS) at Ca’ Foscari Venezia University. Additionally, I am a teacher assistant at a private school in Amsterdam, where I continually refine my teaching methods and interact with students from diverse backgrounds.

Explore Our Diverse Italian Courses

Italian Culture Course: Perfect for those starting their Italian journey or looking to deepen their cultural understanding. This course uses a variety of engaging teaching methods including multimedia resources, situational exercises, and role-playing games.

Conversational Italian: Tailored for learners who wish to enhance their speaking skills. This course focuses on practical speaking exercises involving everyday conversations, ensuring you can communicate effectively in Italian.

Business Italian: Ideal for professionals needing to master Italian for work. This course covers advanced language skills and specific jargon used in business contexts, enhancing your professional communication skills.

Italian for Children: Designed to make learning Italian fun and interactive for younger learners. Through games, songs, and engaging activities, children build a strong foundation in the language.

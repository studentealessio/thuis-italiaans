Frequently asked questions

What level of Italian do I need for these books?

Each book states its CEFR level on the cover and in the listing: from A1 for the easiest readers up to C1 for the most complex novels. If you’re starting out, pick an A1 title and work your way up.

Are these the original texts or simplified versions?

They are adaptations: the plot and spirit of the original remain, but vocabulary and syntax are rewritten for the stated level, with footnotes and a glossary for the hardest words.

Where can I buy them?

On Amazon, as paperback or Kindle ebook. All titles are collected on Alessio Insegnante’s author page.

Prefer reading in the app?

The same novels, with tap-to-translate vocabulary in 14 languages and animated scenes, live inside ti.
